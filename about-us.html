What is PMI?

Precious Medical International (PMI) is Malawi's fastest growing medical insurance company offering top quality products and affordable premiums to cater to all income levels of its members. The PMI scheme is crafted as Best Value for money, which has allowed many organizations to save on revenue while staff and their dependents experience great services in our wide network of hospitals and clinics throughout Malawi and abroad
